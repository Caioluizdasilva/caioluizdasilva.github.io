C++

Since the 1990s it is one of the most popular commercial languages, being widely used also in academia for its great performance and user base.

Bjarne Stroustrup developed C++ (originally named C with Classes, which means C with classes in Portuguese) in 1983 at Bell Labs as an addition to the C language. After iso standardization in 1998 and the subsequent revision in 2003, a new version of the language specification was released in December 2014, informally known as C++17.
